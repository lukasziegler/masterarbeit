Pervasive Displays

With this installation we aim at studying various research questions of displays in the public, amongst others how they can be used to facilitate conducting surveys.

Support us, answer our surveys

Research at LMU Munich

In the Group for Media Informatics at the University of Munich Prof. Dr. Florian Alt studies how people can be enticed to interact in public spaces, and how novel interaction techniques can be created for open pervasive displays. His research interest is at the crossroads of ubiquitous computing and HCI. Find out more on florian-alt.org.

PDSurvey Plattform

With this installation at the LMU Munich (Oettingenstraße 67) we aim to simplify the conception, execution and evaluation of questionnaires. Our goal is to create a research plattform allowing display providers and fellow researchers to execute surveys on their public display networks. Here you see the frontend of the survey plattform, developed by Lukas Ziegler as part of his Master's thesis.

Collected Data

All of the collected data is used for research purposes exclusively and saved anonymously. For this survey plattform we only log your responses which you explicitly submit via the survey tool.
