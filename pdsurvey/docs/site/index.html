Welcome to PDSurvey

PDSurvey is a survey platform for public displays, developed by Lukas Ziegler at the Chair for Media Informatics Group at the University of Munich. The foundation for this platform was laid in the Master thesis supervised by Florian Alt: "Design and Development of a Public Display Survey Platform" The goal of the platform is to facilitate the execution of surveys on public displays and is a toolset for further PD evaluation.
